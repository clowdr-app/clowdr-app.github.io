Everything you need to deliver a great conference

Clowdr is an all-in-one solution for your next virtual conference. We know how much work it is to organise a conference. Clowdr is designed to make your conference platform 'just work' - so that you can concentrate on the things that really matter.

Advanced scheduling

Clowdr supports the complex, multi-track conference programs common in large academic conferences.

Video rooms

Attendees can create ad-hoc video breakout rooms at the click of a button.

Text chat

A familiar and interactive chat experience. Have a rich conversation with polls, Q&A, emoji and more.

Audio-visual production

Our end-to-end video production workflow seamlessly combines pre-recorded videos with live presentations, panel sessions and Q&A.

Attendee networking

Get your attendees talking with shuffle spaces, a way to create spontaneous group conversations.

Sponsor booths

Create custom video booths for sponsors and exhibitors at your conference.

Exhibition hall

Build a virtual exhibition hall to show posters or an important collection of conference items.

Content management

Clowdr helps you gather and manage all the content at your conference, including papers, abstracts, links and videos.

Custom homepage

Welcome attendees to your conference with a custom homepage.

Expert support

Our experienced team is ready to make your conference a success.

A community-owned platform

Clowdr CIC is a social enterprise dedicated to making virtual conferences better for everyone.

All improvements we make to Clowdr become part of the open source platform that anybody can use freely.

Interested in using Clowdr?

There are lots of ways to use Clowdr. Most conferences choose to use our fully hosted 'click-and-go' solution. But you can also run Clowdr yourself at no cost.
