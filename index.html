Running a virtual conference can be hard work

Clowdr makes it easy. After importing your conference program, it's just a couple of clicks to ask presenters to upload their videos, slides or other information.

Clowdr automatically generates subtitles for videos uploaded by your presenters and authors. After subtitling is complete, Clowdr will email the uploader to notify them that they can now make any corrections they want to.

Accessibility is one of Clowdr's core values. Our platform is designed to work well with screen readers and keyboard navigation. Clowdr also supports video subtitling for your pre-recorded content, and live subtitling is coming soon.

We believe that, today, text-based platforms are fundamentally more accessible than trying to replicate a conference in a 2D or 3D virtual environment.

We believe that social connections are the most important thing made at your conference. Attendees can personalise their profile with a picture, badges and a bio, and that means it's always just a click away to find out more about someone you've just met. Clowdr also shows you who's in the room with you, which makes the conference feel like a real shared experience.

Clowdr's powerful chat system includes features your attendees expect, like emoji reactions, Q&A and polls. Create themed video meeting rooms for your attendees to meet new people or discuss a particular topic. Networking sessions run great with our Shuffle feature, which groups attendees together randomly for a limited period of time.

Clowdr is designed to work at every scale—from a single track event through to a multi-week conference with many parallel tracks. When your conference has a lot of content and rapid-fire presentations, it can get difficult to manage. Clowdr shows you the right thing at the right time.

Clowdr's Live Sidebar shows you what's happening right now: who is chatting in a video room? What presentations are coming up next? How many people are in the auditorium right now?

You can create a custom landing page for your conference, organising your content in the way that makes sense for your conference. And Clowdr's Schedule page compresses even a large program into an easy-to-navigate timeline.

Clowdr solves these problems and more, giving you time to focus on the thing that really matters: bringing people together.

Clowdr makes it simple to get live or pre-recorded video from your presenters and make it available to your attendees.

Here's how it works.

Call for content

Import your program, then send requests for authors and presenters to upload their videos (and other content). Authors can edit subtitles that are generated for their videos.

Review and approve uploaded content.

Pre-publish

Optionally publish your pre-recorded videos a week in advance of the conference.

Attendees can use Clowdr chat to discuss your content. Now is a great time to gather questions for authors ahead of the live event.

Go live!

Clowdr turns your conference program into a fully-automated live stream that switches seamlessly between pre-recorded content and live sessions.

You control who has access to the backstage area, and can give your session chairs live control over the layout of the video.

Post-publish and archive

Your live-streamed events are automatically recorded by Clowdr so that attendees can watch or rewatch them later.

Choose whether to publish your pre-recorded content and live recordings directly to YouTube, including subtitles.

Why go virtual with Clowdr?

Built for your event

Clowdr was founded by academics with decades of combined experience organising large, complex academic conferences. It handles everything from simple seminars to week-long, multi-track conferences with thousands of attendees.

By the community, for the community

Clowdr is a social enterprise, incorporated as a Community Interest Company. Our mission is to make inclusive, accessible, climate-friendly virtual conferences just as socially engaging as physical conferences.

Proudly open source

If you want to do-it-yourself, you can run your very own Clowdr. We also support researchers who want to modify Clowdr or use it in their research.

What we offer

Click-and-go

We worry about the technology; you organize the conference. Use Clowdr - hosted by the people who built it.

Expert support and advice

We can advise you on how to plan and set up your conference and provide virtual on-site support to make sure everything runs smoothly.

Advanced customisation

Want to wow your attendees with something new? We can design and build it.

Do-it-yourself

Want to run the Clowdr platform yourself? We can help you with that.
